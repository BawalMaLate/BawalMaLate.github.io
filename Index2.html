Bawal Ma-Late!

Bawal Ma-LATE! is a single PC multiplayer game with 3D graphics available on the Windows platform, with themes of urban commuting and transportation to showcase some of the commuters' inconveniences in the form of a digital board game where there are five characters to choose from, played with a maximum of four players as they compete with each other in order to reach as many destination areas as possible.

About the Meeples

Rosa/Nurse

Rosa is an experienced nurse who commutes daily from her residence in Calamba, Laguna to her workplace in Quezon City. She has been making this commute for the past 27 years. However, today is different as there is an urgent emergency, and assistance is sought to ensure she reaches her destination on time.

Meesha/Student

Meesha is a college student who lives in Cainta, Rizal, and commutes daily to school. Due to the ongoing maintenance of the LRT, Meesha has to wake up early and take a crowded bus to reach her school on time. This particular day is important for her as she has an upcoming exam. Assistance is sought to ensure that Meesha reaches his destination successfully.

Ray/Office worker

Ray is a fresh graduate and office worker residing in Meycauayan, Bulacan. Every day, he travels from his home to his office in Ortigas by taking two trains, namely LRT1 and MRT3. Unfortunately, he often experiences overcrowded trains during his morning commute. Today, Ray has an important meeting, and he seeks assistance to ensure he reaches his destination on time.

Jose/Construction worker

Jose is a devoted single parent living in San Jose Del Monte, Bulacan, with three children. He works tirelessly to secure a better future for his family. Every day, he endures an exhausting commute of nearly 10 hours, leaving him with little time for rest when he returns home. Unfortunately, one day he oversleeps and needs assistance to ensure he reaches his destination on time.

Mary/Parent

A dedicated housewife, managing the household chores and takes care of her family. She has her own daily routine, which involved commuting and vising the local market. But one day there was a transportation strike, and public jeepneys very few, the only option is for her to walk or ride a bicycle. Will you be able to help Mary reach her destination ?
